Who We Are & What We Do

The Makhanda SPCA (formerly Grahamstown SPCA) has protected animals since 1958 and has been a registered Non-Profit Organisation (NPO 035-579) since 2004. We are independent, receive no government funding, and rely on donations, our Charity Shop, and community support to continue our vital work of animal welfare and protection.

How We are Funded: Your support powers everything we do. Donations, Charity Shop purchases, and local fundraising cover essential care, inspections, rescues, and outreach programs. Every contribution, no matter the size, directly impacts the lives of animals in need.

How We Operate: A volunteer Management Committee oversees the Society, while dedicated staff run our kennels, inspectorate services, and charity shop. Volunteers are welcomed and play a crucial role in our daily operations, from animal care to administrative support.

Standards & Affiliation: We are affiliated with the NSPCA and comply with national animal welfare standards, ensuring consistent, ethical care. Our commitment to excellence means every animal receives the highest standard of treatment and compassion.
