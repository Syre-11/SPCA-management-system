##Makanda SPCA Management System
Overview

The Makanda SPCA Management System is a web-based information system developed to support the operational needs of the Society for the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals (SPCA). The system centralises animal welfare management, adoption processes, cruelty reporting, community engagement, and donation tracking into a single integrated platform to improve efficiency, data accuracy, and decision-making.

The platform provides role-based access for administrative staff, veterinary staff, and volunteers, while allowing the public to view animals and submit adoption applications without requiring authentication.

System Objectives

Improve the management of animal intake, care, and welfare

Support the reporting and investigation of animal cruelty cases

Facilitate responsible animal adoption and rehoming

Promote community engagement through volunteering and fostering

Manage donations and fundraising activities

Provide operational insights through reporting and analytics

System Users
Administrative Staff

Full access to all system functionality and data

Veterinary Staff

Access to animal records and medical information

Restricted from modifying adoption-related data

Volunteer Staff

View animal profiles

Manage volunteer interactions and activities

Public Users

View SPCA information and available animals

Submit adoption applications (no login required)

Core Features
Animal Intake and Registration

Register stray, rescued, or surrendered animals

Capture species, breed, health status, and rescue details

Assign animals to compatible kennel spaces

Medical Care Management

Record vaccinations, treatments, and procedures

Track spaying and neutering status

Store veterinarian details and procedure dates

Monitor ongoing animal health

Cruelty Reporting and Investigation

Capture cruelty reports including date, description, and evidence

Link reports to specific animals

Notify staff when new reports are submitted

Adoption and Rehoming

Maintain profiles of adoptable animals

Manage adoption applications and screening processes

Approve or reject adopters

Track post-adoption follow-ups

Community Engagement

Register volunteers

Track volunteer activities and hours

Manage foster programs and educational initiatives

Fundraising and Donation Management

Capture donor information

Track donations and pledges

Flag high-value donations for acknowledgement

Generate donation receipts

Public Access

View SPCA information

Browse animals available for adoption

Submit online adoption applications

Reporting and Analytics

Monitor animal intake and adoption rates

Track kennel occupancy and resource utilisation

Analyse donation trends

View volunteer participation statistics

Business Rules
Animal Registration

Each animal has a unique system identifier

Kennels have defined capacity and species restrictions

Animals may only be assigned to compatible kennels

Cruelty Reporting

Each report must include a date and detailed description

Each report is linked to exactly one animal

An animal may have multiple cruelty reports

Medical Care

Each medical procedure must include a veterinarian and date

Animals must be vaccinated and spayed/neutered before adoption

Adoption Rules

Animals cannot be adopted until all medical treatment is complete

An animal may have only one adopter

An adopter may adopt multiple animals

Donation Management

Each donation is linked to a single donor

Donation date and value are mandatory

High-value donations are flagged for administrative review

User Permissions

Administrators have full system access

Veterinarians have medical access only

Public users have view-only access and can submit adoption applications

System Notifications

Automatic notifications are sent to staff when:

New cruelty reports are submitted

High-value donations are received

Critical animal care updates occur
